3.2  DEPARTURE BRIEFING
Prior to Departure Briefing
Prior to departure briefing, both Flight Crew members shall review and verify the following:
  • All relevant data / NOTAMS / OM Part C / Notes on Jeppesen Charts / SCB / FCOM Bulletins / OEBs;
  • Aerodrome Special Procedures (e.g. NADP);
  • FMS setup checked against Departure Clearance, OFP and Published Procedures by PF and PM:
    • From FMS: Runway / SID / Transition / Altitude and Speed Restrictions
    • From Chart: SID Designator / Initial Altitude or Level / Altitude and Speed Restrictions / Transition Altitude / Terrain
